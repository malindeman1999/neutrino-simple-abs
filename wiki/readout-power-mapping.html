Equation 3 Mapping

Using the symmetric-resonance relation from the resonator-bolometer paper (Eq. 3), the model maps generator power to dissipated power as:

\[ P_0 = \frac{1}{2}\,\frac{1}{1+4Q_r^2x^2}\, \frac{4Q_cQ_i}{(Q_c+Q_i)^2}\,P_g. \]

Definitions in this codebase:

  • \(P_g\): generator/readout drive power (`Pg_W`), set by \((P_g/P_{bif})_{target}\).
  • \(P_0\): resonator-dissipated power (`P0_W`), used for island heating and \(\Delta T_{abs-bath}\).
  • \(Q_r, Q_i, Q_c\): loaded, internal, and coupling quality factors.
  • \(x\): dimensionless detuning, \(x\equiv \delta f/f_0\).
