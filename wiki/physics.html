Critical Timescale Hierarchy

Required ordering:

\(\tau_{qp} \ll \tau_{res} \ll \tau_{th}\)

This ordering is used as the design-rule basis for stable operation and pulse reconstruction.

Resonator Dynamics

\(\tau_{res} = \dfrac{Q}{\pi f_0}\)

Constraint: \(\tau_{res} \le \tau_{th}/3\)

Implications:

  • Q cannot be arbitrarily high.
  • \(f_0\) must remain in the GHz band.
  • Resonator bandwidth must preserve pulse shape.

Thermal Physics

\(\tau_{th} = C/G\)

Coupling hierarchy requirement:

\(G_{AK} \ge 10\,G_{AB}\) and therefore \(G_{AK} \gg G_{AB}\).

This enforces single-node thermal behavior and suppresses internal thermal fluctuation noise.

Code-derived model formulas used by this wiki:

\[ \tau_{\mathrm{th}}=\frac{C}{G},\qquad G=\frac{C}{\tau_{\mathrm{th}}},\qquad \Delta T_{\mathrm{abs-bath}}=\frac{P_0}{G},\qquad \Delta T_{\mathrm{event}}=\frac{E_{\mathrm{Ho}}}{C},\qquad E_{\mathrm{Ho,eV}}=\frac{E_{\mathrm{Ho}}}{q_e},\qquad C_{\mathrm{eV/mK}}=\frac{C}{q_e\cdot 10^3},\qquad C_{\mathrm{Ho,eV/mK}}=\frac{C_{\mathrm{Ho}}}{q_e\cdot 10^3},\qquad P_{\mathrm{ph,RMS}}=\sqrt{4k_BT_0^2G\,F_{\mathrm{link}}(T_0,T_b,n)}. \]

Pileup now uses an NEP-sufficiency timing window derived from the high-to-low cumulative NEP integral:

\[ \sigma_E^{\mathrm{hi\to lo}}(f)=\left[\int_{f}^{f_{\max}}\frac{4}{\mathrm{NEP}(f')^2}\,df'\right]^{-1/2},\qquad \sigma_{E,\mathrm{full}}=\left[\int_{f_{\min}}^{f_{\max}}\frac{4}{\mathrm{NEP}(f')^2}\,df'\right]^{-1/2}, \]

\[ f_{\mathrm{NEP,suff}}=\max\left\{f:\sigma_E^{\mathrm{hi\to lo}}(f)\le\left(1+\frac{p_{\mathrm{NEP,suff}}}{100}\right)\sigma_{E,\mathrm{full}}\right\},\qquad \tau_{\mathrm{NEP,suff}}=\frac{1}{f_{\mathrm{NEP,suff}}}, \]

\[ P_{\mathrm{pileup,reject}}=1-e^{-2R\tau_{\mathrm{NEP,suff}}}. \]

This is a per-event rejection probability for a \(\pm\tau_{\mathrm{NEP,suff}}\) coincidence veto, where both colliding pulses are rejected.

Python: Sensor.tau_th_s, Sensor.G_W_per_K, Sensor.phonon_power_rms_W.

Leg Geometry Formula

With leg thickness tied to membrane thickness, leg length is derived from target conductance:

\[ t_{\mathrm{leg}}=t_{\mathrm{mem}},\qquad L_{\mathrm{leg}}=\frac{N_{\mathrm{leg}}\kappa_{\mathrm{leg}}w_{\mathrm{leg}}t_{\mathrm{leg}}}{G}. \]

Python: Sensor.leg_thickness_m, Sensor.leg_length_m.

Energy Resolution Model

Baseline relation:

\(\Delta E \approx \sqrt{k_B T^2 C}\)

With responsivity alpha:

\(\Delta E \propto \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{\alpha}}\sqrt{k_B T^2 C}\)

TLS Frequency Noise

TLS contribution scaling:

\(\dfrac{S_f}{f^2} \propto \dfrac{F\,\tan(\delta_{TLS})}{f^{\beta}}\)

TLS depends on capacitor geometry and dielectric quality, not on thermal design.

Design condition: keep fractional frequency noise below the phonon-noise-equivalent floor over the analysis band.
