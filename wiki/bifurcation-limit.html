Bifurcation Model

We use the standard kinetic-inductance resonator scaling:

\[ P_{\mathrm{bif}}=\frac{Q_c\omega_0E_*}{2Q_r^3}, \qquad Q_c=\left(\frac{1}{Q_r}-\frac{1}{Q_i}\right)^{-1}, \qquad \omega_0=2\pi f_0. \]

Here \(E_*\) is the nonlinear energy scale (model input).

Operating Margin

Define the proximity ratio:

\[ \frac{P_0}{P_{\mathrm{bif}}}. \]
