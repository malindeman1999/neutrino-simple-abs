Frequency Context

Project baseline is \(f_c\approx1\,\mathrm{GHz}\) with 1000 resonators/chip and \(\Delta f/f=0.002\). IDC sizing should maintain stable resonance placement while minimizing electric-field concentration.

Capacitance Target

Use total resonator capacitance in the approximate range:

\[ C_{tot}\approx0.5\text{ to }2\,\mathrm{pF} \]

with larger values preferred where area allows, to reduce readout voltage and TLS drive.

Layout Priorities for Low TLS

  1. Keep strongest fields in vacuum/air regions, not lossy interfaces.
  2. Avoid deposited dielectric overlap in high-field IDC regions.
  3. Use larger capacitor area to reduce voltage for given resonator energy.
  4. Use symmetric IDC routing and rounded corner transitions where possible.

Design Flow

  1. Pick \(L\) and \(C\) for 1 GHz center design window.
  2. Generate IDC geometry with large gaps and area-heavy capacitance.
  3. Extract participation \(F\) from EM simulation.
  4. Measure a resonator set vs \(P_{int}\) and fit TLS parameters.
  5. Refine geometry to hit noise floor and multiplexing yield.
