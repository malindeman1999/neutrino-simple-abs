KID Heat Capacity at 100 mK

Estimate using the same low-temperature specific heat model as superconducting capping.

Assumptions

Use the same phonon specific heat estimate as capping at \(100\,\mathrm{mK}\):

\[ c_{KID}(100\,\mathrm{mK})\approx c_{cap}(100\,\mathrm{mK})\approx 3.0\times10^{-10}\,\mathrm{J/(g\,K)}. \]

Use WSi-like density \(\rho\approx 9.3\,\mathrm{g/cm^3}\).

Example KID footprint and thickness for island budgeting:

\[ A_{KID}=30\times30=900\,\mu\mathrm{m}^2, \quad t_{KID}=0.05\,\mu\mathrm{m}\ (50\,\mathrm{nm}). \]

Volume and Heat Capacity

\[ V_{KID}=A_{KID}t_{KID}=45\,\mu\mathrm{m}^3=4.5\times10^{-17}\,\mathrm{m}^3. \]

\[ C_{KID}(100\,\mathrm{mK})\approx 1.26\times10^{-19}\,\mathrm{J/K}. \]

Converted units:

\[ C_{KID}(100\,\mathrm{mK})\approx 7.8\times10^{-4}\,\mathrm{eV/mK}. \]

Thickness Range

For \(t_{KID}=30{-}100\,\mathrm{nm}\) with the same area and material model:

\[ C_{KID}(100\,\mathrm{mK})\approx 4.7\times10^{-4}\text{ to }1.6\times10^{-3}\,\mathrm{eV/mK}. \]

This remains negligible versus Ho-source heat capacity at the current design level.
